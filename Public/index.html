About Me

My dream job is to work remote and enjoy as much time outside as possible. I think that this bootcamp is the first step to living that dream. I know there will be a lot of tough times ahead, staring at the screen with ill intent, but I look forward to the reward on the otherside. To me coding is like hiking to the summit of a mounatin. The incline never stops, you reach a few false peaks when you think you've finally made it, and you continually doubt yourself. But then you make it and the view is glorius. You suddenly forget about all of struggle and its pure bliss. Then its on to the next one!
